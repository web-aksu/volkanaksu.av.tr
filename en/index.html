Mergers & Acquisitions

Mergers and acquisitions are among the most critical steps in company growth and restructuring processes. These transactions involve not only the analysis of financial statements, but also identifying legal risks, ensuring compliance with sector-specific regulations, and determining accurate commercial strategies.

Our firm provides comprehensive legal support in mergers and acquisitions. We carry out due diligence processes, prepare contracts that protect the interests of buyers or sellers, and closely monitor negotiations. In complex multinational transactions, we offer detailed consultancy to ensure compliance with both local legislation and international regulations.

We create a strategic roadmap tailored to our clients' objectives, ensuring that transactions are completed safely, efficiently, and effectively. Notifications and approvals required by the Competition Authority and other regulatory bodies are also followed by our team, securing every stage of the process.

Mediation

Mediation is an alternative dispute resolution method aimed at resolving legal conflicts between parties amicably, without the need for court proceedings. Our firm provides professional support in both mandatory and voluntary mediation processes.

Our experienced team specializes in a wide range of disputes, including commercial conflicts, employer–employee relations, consumer complaints, rental issues, and partnership disagreements, offering fair and lasting solutions that protect the interests of all parties.

During mediation, we facilitate communication between parties and contribute to achieving agreements in a confidential and secure environment. We focus on obtaining the most efficient outcome while providing time and cost advantages for our clients.

Criminal Law

Criminal law is one of the most critical legal fields, directly affecting fundamental rights and freedoms. Our firm supports clients at every stage of criminal proceedings, developing effective defense strategies throughout investigation, prosecution, and appeal processes.

We offer expertise in a wide range of areas including economic crimes, cybercrimes, corporate liability, and personal offenses. Allegations, evidence, and procedural steps are examined in detail, ensuring strong defense against unjust accusations.

Protecting the rights of victims is also a key priority for our firm. We pursue legal actions to ensure compensation for damages arising from criminal acts and strive to achieve justice effectively.

Family Law

Family law is not only a legal field but also one with strong social and emotional dimensions. In divorce, custody, alimony, property division, and parentage cases, we adopt an approach that considers both legal and emotional needs of our clients.

The best interests of the child are our primary concern in family disputes. We take necessary legal steps to protect children's psychological and social development in custody and visitation arrangements. In divorce proceedings, we produce solutions that protect the rights of both parties and minimize potential conflicts.

Before litigation, we encourage amicable resolution methods such as mediation and settlement. However, when disputes proceed to court, we provide strong legal representation to achieve the most just outcome for our clients.

Enforcement & Bankruptcy Law

One of the most common issues in commercial relations is the inability to collect receivables. Our firm effectively conducts enforcement proceedings to ensure creditors recover their rights as quickly as possible. We also provide legal support for debt restructuring and the use of legal mechanisms such as concordat for debtor clients.

Enforcement and bankruptcy processes include not only litigation, but also preventive consultancy services. We advise on collateral provisions in contracts, mortgages, and pledge transactions to secure commercial relationships.

Our firm produces legal solutions to protect company assets during bankruptcy, liquidation, and concordat processes. Our goal is to minimize financial losses and help maintain sustainable commercial balance.

Commercial Law

Commercial activities are subject to numerous legal regulations both during company formation and operational stages. Our firm provides preventive legal consultancy and dispute resolution services in all areas of commercial law.

We offer legal guidance on company establishment, share transfers, board resolutions, and maintenance of commercial books. We protect our clients’ interests in drafting, negotiating, and implementing commercial contracts, including distribution, dealership, supply, partnership, and franchise agreements.

In case of disputes, we represent our clients in commercial litigation and pursue all legal remedies to protect their rights. Our aim is to ensure uninterrupted and secure commercial operations while strengthening our clients’ competitive position.

Inheritance Law

Inheritance law regulates how a person’s estate is transferred after death, often involving complex family dynamics and disputes. Therefore, the process must be handled with precision and sensitivity.

Our firm provides comprehensive legal support in inheritance distribution, will preparation and execution, inheritance agreements, infringement of reserved portions, reduction (tenkis) lawsuits, and renunciation of inheritance.

Our goal is to prevent family conflicts and ensure that inheritance is distributed fairly and in accordance with the law. Throughout the process, we protect our clients’ rights and offer swift and lasting legal solutions.

Lease Law

Lease law regulates the rights and obligations arising from rental agreements between landlords and tenants. Our firm provides legal consultancy for both residential and commercial leases, ensuring the protection of our clients’ interests.

We offer services in drafting and renewing lease agreements, determining rental amounts, eviction lawsuits, adaptation actions, and rent increase disputes.

Our objective is to establish strong contractual relationships, prevent potential disputes, and ensure the quickest possible resolution when conflicts arise.

Labor Law

Labor law regulates the relationship between employees and employers and forms the foundation of working life. Our firm provides professional legal support for both parties in all employment-related matters.

We offer representation in wrongful termination claims, severance and notice compensation, overtime disputes, mobbing cases, reinstatement lawsuits, contract arrangements, and social security conflicts.

Our aim is to maintain workplace harmony and produce fair solutions that balance the rights of both employees and employers. We prioritize efficient dispute resolution within the framework of preventive legal practices.

Insurance Law

Insurance law governs the rights and obligations arising from insurance contracts. Our firm provides comprehensive legal services in disputes involving both insured parties and insurance companies.

We represent our clients in insurance compensation claims, policy cancellations, risk assessments, damage evaluations, and recourse actions.

Our objective is to protect the rights arising from insurance contracts and ensure full compensation for our clients’ losses.

Real Estate Law

Real estate law covers all legal transactions related to immovable property. Our firm offers a wide range of consultancy services, from real estate purchases and sales to urban transformation projects.

We have extensive experience in title cancellation and registration lawsuits, condominium disputes, lease relationships, construction contracts based on land share, and contractor receivables.

Our goal is to ensure legally compliant real estate investments and protect our clients’ rights effectively in potential disputes.
