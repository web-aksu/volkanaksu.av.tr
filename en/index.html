Mergers and Acquisitions

Mergers and acquisition transactions are one of the most important steps in companies' growth and restructuring processes. These processes consist not only of examining financial statements but also carry great importance in terms of detecting legal risks, complying with sectoral regulations, and correctly determining commercial strategies.

Our firm provides comprehensive legal support to its clients in merger and acquisition transactions. In this context, due diligence processes are conducted, contracts are prepared to protect the interests of the buyer or seller, and negotiations are meticulously followed. Detailed consultancy is provided especially in complex multinational transactions to ensure compliance with both local legislation and international regulations.

By creating a roadmap suitable for our clients' strategic goals, transactions are ensured to be completed securely, quickly, and effectively. Notifications and permissions required before the Competition Authority and other regulatory authorities are also followed up by us, securing every stage of the process legally.

Mediation

Mediation is an alternative dispute resolution method aiming to resolve legal disputes amicably between parties without the need for court proceedings. Our firm offers professional support to clients in both mandatory and voluntary mediation processes.

Our team, specializing in a wide range of areas such as commercial disputes, employer-employee relations, consumer complaints, lease, and partnership disagreements, aims to produce fair and permanent solutions considering the interests of the parties.

In the mediation process, we facilitate communication between parties and contribute to reaching an agreement in a secure environment based on confidentiality. In this process, which provides time and cost advantages, we focus on obtaining the most efficient result while protecting our clients' rights.

Criminal Law

Criminal law is one of the most critical branches of law directly concerning the fundamental rights and freedoms of individuals. Our firm stands by its clients at all stages of criminal proceedings; developing an effective defense strategy during investigation, prosecution, and appeal processes.

We offer expertise in a wide spectrum such as economic crimes, cybercrimes, liabilities of company executives, or personal crimes. Allegations, evidence, and procedural actions faced by our clients are examined in the finest detail; an effective defense is made against unjust accusations.

Additionally, protecting the rights of clients in the victim position is among our firm's priority services. In this context, legal initiatives are taken for the compensation of damages arising from the crime, aiming to ensure justice is served most effectively.

Family Law

Family law is not just a legal field, but also one with a strong social and human dimension. In divorce, custody, alimony, property division, and paternity cases, we adopt an approach that considers both the legal and emotional needs of our clients.

The best interest of the child is our primary priority in family law disputes. In custody and visitation arrangements, necessary legal steps are taken to protect the child's psychological and social development. In divorce processes, solutions are generated to protect the parties' rights and minimize potential conflicts.

Our firm brings up amicable resolution methods like mediation and reconciliation before resorting to litigation. However, if disputes move to court, we provide comprehensive legal representation to defend our clients' rights determinedly and reach the fairest outcome.

Bankruptcy and Enforcement Law

One of the most common problems in commercial relations is the inability to collect receivables. Our firm effectively carries out enforcement proceedings for creditors to obtain their rights as soon as possible. Besides this, we provide legal support to debtor clients regarding debt restructuring and benefiting from legal possibilities like concordat.

Enforcement and bankruptcy processes consist not only of the litigation stage but also cover preventive consultancy services. Consultancy is provided on preventive measures such as guarantee provisions to be placed in contracts, mortgage, and pledge transactions to secure our clients' commercial relations.

Our firm produces legal solutions aimed at protecting company assets in processes like bankruptcy postponement, liquidation, and concordat. Our goal is to help our clients maintain a sustainable commercial balance by minimizing their economic losses.

Commercial Law

Commercial life is subject to numerous legal regulations both during the establishment stage of companies and while sustaining their activities. Our firm offers both preventive consultancy and dispute resolution services to its clients in all areas of commercial law.

We provide legal guidance on issues such as company incorporations, share transfers, board resolutions, and keeping commercial books. We act to protect our clients' interests in the preparation, negotiation, and implementation processes of commercial contracts. Detailed legal support is provided especially in agreements forming the basis of commercial life such as distributorship, dealership, supply, partnership, and franchise contracts.

In case of disputes, we represent our clients in commercial lawsuits and apply to all necessary legal remedies to protect their rights. Our aim is to ensure commercial activities are maintained uninterruptedly and in confidence, and to increase our clients' competitive power.

Inheritance Law

Inheritance law is the branch of law regulating to whom and how a person's assets will pass after their death. Since this area often becomes a subject of dispute among family members, it is a process that must be carried out meticulously.

Our firm offers comprehensive legal support to clients on issues such as division of inheritance, preparation and execution of wills, inheritance contracts, violation of reserved shares, abatement lawsuits, and refusal of inheritance.

Our aim is to prevent family disputes and ensure the inheritance is shared fairly and in accordance with the law. Throughout the process, we protect our clients' rights and produce fast and permanent solutions.

Lease Law

Lease law regulates the rights and obligations arising from the contract between the tenant and the lessor. Our firm offers legal consultancy protecting the interests of parties in both residential and workplace rentals.

We provide effective services in areas such as preparation and renewal of lease agreements, determination of rental fees, eviction lawsuits, adaptation, and rent increase lawsuits.

Our goal is to place the contractual relationship between parties on solid ground, prevent potential disagreements in advance, and provide solutions as soon as possible in case of disputes.

Labor Law

Labor law is a branch of law regulating the relationship between employee and employer and forming the basis of working life. Our firm provides professional support in all kinds of legal processes for both employee and employer sides.

We offer representation services with our experienced team in areas such as unfair dismissal, severance and notice pay, overtime, mobbing, re-employment lawsuits, contract arrangements, and SSI disputes.

Our aim is to produce a fair solution balancing the rights of both the employee and the employer by preserving labor peace. We take care to resolve disputes most efficiently within the framework of preventive law.

Insurance Law

Insurance law is an important legal field regulating the determination of rights and debts arising from insurance contracts. Our firm offers comprehensive legal services in disputes where both the insured and insurance companies are parties.

We represent our clients with our expert team in issues such as insurance compensation lawsuits, policy cancellation, risk assessment, damage assessment, and recourse lawsuits.

Our aim is the protection of rights arising from the insurance contract and the complete compensation of damages suffered by our clients.

Real Estate Law

Real estate law covers all kinds of legal transactions related to immovable property. Our firm offers consultancy services in a wide range extending from real estate trading to urban transformation projects.

We have experience in title deed cancellation and registration cases, condominium disputes, lease relations, construction contracts in return for land share, and contractor receivables.

Our goal is to ensure real estate investments are carried out in accordance with the law and to protect our clients' rights most effectively in potential disputes.
